Suicide Around The World

Top 10 Country in Suicide Cases

In this visualisation, it shows the top 10 countries that having the most number of suicide cases. From the visualisation, it is clear that Russia is having the highest number. It also having high value of suicide rate which every 100,000 people, there will be 33 people will commit suicide.

Suicide Rate from Different Continent

This chart shows the suicide rate in different continent. It is clear to see that Europe is having the highest suicide rate among all the continents. In the previous visualisation, it shows Russia having the highest number of suicides case and Russia is one of the country from Europe, therefore it does really affect the suicide rate of Europe.

Suicide Rate between Gender in Different Continent

This chart shows the suicide rate from different gender and continent. From the visualistion, male has more suicide cases compare to female. This can be related as male could be more stressful than female in different field. Both male and female in Europe having the highest suicide rate from this visualisation.
